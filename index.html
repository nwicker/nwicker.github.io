Summary

Nate Wicker grew up in the Washington, D.C. area. He attended the University of Michigan where he studied finance and graduated from the Ross School of Business. Following college, Nate spent a year in China as a teacher. Then he worked in the energy industry in Houston and London for an investment bank, operating company and family office. Additionally, Nate co-founded a cash payments and data analytics startup. Nate is now a student in Chicago at Kellogg. He is an avid rock climber and history enthusiast.
